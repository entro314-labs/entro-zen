---
title: Text
---

A text element with controls for size, color, weight and letter spacing.

<Example>
  <Text>Here is some text.</Text>
</Example>

## Colors

<Example>
  <Text>Normal text</Text>
  <Text color="muted">Muted text</Text>
  <Text color="disabled">Disabled text</Text>
  <Text color="red">Colored text</Text>
</Example>

<Example>
  <Text size="1">Size 1</Text>
  <Text size="2">Size 2</Text>
  <Text size="3">Size 3</Text>
  <Text size="4">Size 4</Text>
  <Text size="5">Size 5</Text>
  <Text size="6">Size 6</Text>
  <Text size="7">Size 7</Text>
  <Text size="8">Size 8</Text>
  <Text size="9">Size 9</Text>
  <Text size="10">Size 10</Text>
  <Text size="11">Size 11</Text>
  <Text size="12">Size 12</Text>
</Example>

## Weights

<Example>
  <Text weight="light">Light text</Text>
  <Text weight="normal">Normal text</Text>
  <Text weight="medium">Medium text</Text>
  <Text weight="bold">Bold text</Text>
</Example>

## Alignment

<Example>
  <Box width="100%">
    <Text align="left" as="div">
      {'Left'}
    </Text>
    <Text align="center" as="div">
      {'Center'}
    </Text>
    <Text align="right" as="div">
      {'Right'}
    </Text>
  </Box>
</Example>

## Letter spacing

<Example>
  <Text spacing="1">Letter spacing 1</Text>
  <Text spacing="2">Letter spacing 2</Text>
  <Text spacing="3">Letter spacing 3 (normal)</Text>
  <Text spacing="4">Letter spacing 4</Text>
  <Text spacing="5">Letter spacing 5</Text>
</Example>

## Formatting

<Example>
  <Text italic>Italic</Text>
  <Text underline>Underline</Text>
  <Text strikethrough>Strikethrough</Text>
</Example>

## Transform

<Example>
  <Text transform="capitalize">Capitalize</Text>
  <Text transform="uppercase">Uppercase</Text>
  <Text transform="lowercase">Lowercase</Text>
</Example>
